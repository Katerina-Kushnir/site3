    About company

    “LeanGroup EUROPE” is young and ambitious company which was founded in 2018 in Poland, Warsaw.
    The main activity of the business – flexible plastic packaging (tubes) for cosmetics (skin care, oral care, hair care), food products (such as dressings, mustard, condensed milk, etс).
    We also offer sachet and labels printing.

    Our sale geography at the mom net contains EU countries as well as GB, but our ambitious and potential to
    work all around the world.
